MycoLog Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 July 2026

MycoLog is an offline cultivation-tracking app. Your privacy is simple to explain because the app is built not to collect anything.

What we collect

Nothing. MycoLog does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. There are no analytics, no advertising, no crash reporting, and no user accounts.

Where your data lives

Everything you enter — batches, stage events, harvests, observations, tasks, photos, and species/substrate library entries — is stored only on your device, in a local database. It is never uploaded to us or to any third party. We have no server, and no way to see your data.

Photos

When you attach a photo, MycoLog copies it into the app's local storage on your device. Photos never leave your device unless you deliberately share them yourself (see Export & Sharing below).

Notifications

Task reminders are local notifications scheduled on your device. They are not push notifications and involve no network connection or third-party service.

Export & sharing

If you use the Export feature, MycoLog creates a backup file (JSON or CSV) and hands it to your device's own share sheet. Where that file goes is entirely your choice — MycoLog does not send it anywhere on its own. Importing a backup reads a file you select; nothing is transmitted.

Permissions

  • Photos / storage access — to let you attach photos and to read a backup file you choose to import.
  • Notifications — to show task reminders. Denying this simply disables reminders; the rest of the app works normally.

Children

MycoLog does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.
